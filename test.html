Eatz Chez Vouz

Located in the West Island's Marché de l'ouest, Eatz Chez Vouz offers a wide variety of gourmet 'prêt-à-manger' meals, baked goods, comfort food and special holiday treats for people on the go and not willing to compromise on what they eat. All our dishes are made on site and cooked fresh daily. Our 'prêt-à-manger' concept caters to all your needs, whether you take your meals home or eat something on the go.

Eatz Chez Vouz also specializes in catering for special events, as well as business functions and offers cooking classes.
